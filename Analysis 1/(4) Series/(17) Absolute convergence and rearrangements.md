$\sum a_n$ [[(9) Absolutely Converges (Series)]] $\iff$ (1) + (2) $\implies$ (3) + (4)
Where:


(1) $\sum_{a_n \ge 0} a_n$ [[(3) Converges (Series)]] (to A)
(2) $\sum_{a_n < 0} a_n$ [[(3) Converges (Series)]] (to B)
(3) $\sum a_n$ = A + B
(4) $\sum b_m$ = A + B where $(b_m)$ is a [[(16) Rearrangement]] of $(a_n)$
